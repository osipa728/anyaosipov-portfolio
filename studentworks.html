Angles Algorithmic Poster Series

Urban Feedback & Resonance

This printed edition consists of three student artist interviews, physically documenting their work and process in their living and working spaces. The interviews and photographic documentation conducted by fellow student, Anya Osipov, in October and November of 2021 explore various social and personal influences on the artists in an effort to capture the renewed creative trajectories of students after the pandemic started. Printed and assembled in May 2022.

The typeface exclusively used in this edition is Self Modern, designed by the Bretagne type foundry. Styles in use are regular and italic.
